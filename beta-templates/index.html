Staff members accessing the system should use their own credentials for updating information. Your PIN should not be disclosed or shared with anyone.

You are about to sign in to the Department of Education and Training's automated child care attendance system. This system has been designed to capture actual attendance data for early childhood services.

Information in this system will be used by the Australian Government for testing purposes only. It will not be replicated or sent to any party outside the Commonwealth and all personal information, including names will be held in accordance with the Privacy Act 1988 (opens in new window).
